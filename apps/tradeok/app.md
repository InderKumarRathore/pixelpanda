## Name

TradeOK

## Description

TradeOK helps employees stay informed about company trading windows and blackout periods.
Instead of digging through policy documents or emails, TradeOK clearly shows when trading is open and when it is restricted—so you always know where things stand.
Key features:
• Clear view of open and closed trading periods
• Calendar-based timeline for blackout periods
• Simple "at-a-glance" trading window status
• Designed for employees with company equity
• Clean, distraction free interface

TradeOK is intended for informational purposes only and does not provide trading approval or legal advice. Always follow your company’s insider trading policy and guidance from your legal or compliance team.

Stay informed. Stay compliant.
